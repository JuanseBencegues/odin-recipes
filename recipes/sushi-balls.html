Sushi Balls

Back to menu

Sushi balls are a charming and delicious variation of traditional sushi, capturing the essence of Japanese cuisine in a single bite. Join us on a journey to discover their history, preparation, and versatility, and how these little delights have become a cherished culinary treasure worldwide. Welcome to the world of sushi balls!

Prep time: 15min

Aditional time: 10min

Total time: 25min

Servings: 6 aprox

How To Make Sushi Balls

You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Sushi Balls Ingredients:

  • An atun can
  • 4 spoons of rice: (rice must be pre-boiled)
  • 2 spoons of mayonaise
  • 1/2 avocado

Directios

  1. Step 1: Mix all the Ingredients in a bowl
  2. Step 2: Once mixed all, make balls with the preapation as big as you want
  3. Step 3: Finally, let them rest at refrigerator for 10min
